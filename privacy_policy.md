# Privacy Policy for Fruitly

**Effective Date:** April 2026
**Developer:** Golden Pineapple Games

This privacy policy applies to the Fruitly app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Golden Pineapple Games (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free-to-Play service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:

* Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
* The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
* The time spent on the Application
* Your generated in-game Usernames and unique User IDs mapped to leaderboard progression.
* In-game behavioral telemetry (e.g., questions answered, items equipped, and buttons clicked) meant strictly to balance mechanics.
* Device identifiers and Advertising IDs (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) used by third parties for advertising purposes.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices, and marketing promotions.

## Third Party Access and Advertising

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help the Service Provider improve the Application and their service. The Application does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

**Personalized Advertising:**
We use third-party advertising companies, such as AdMob, to serve ads. These partners collect and use your Advertising ID, IP address, and other device identifiers to deliver personalized advertisements tailored to your interests based on your activity across different apps and websites. 

**Opting Out of Personalized Ads:**
You have the right to opt out of targeted advertising. You can do this at any time through your device settings:
* **Android:** Go to Settings > Google > Ads > and select "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete advertising ID".

Depending on your region (e.g., the European Economic Area under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you will also be presented with a consent form upon launching the Application to accept or decline personalized advertising before any such tracking occurs.

Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the Application:

* [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## In-App Purchases

Our Application allows you to buy digital items through "In-App Purchases". These transactions are handled directly by Google Play. We do not process or store any of your financial data, credit card numbers, or billing addresses on our servers. All transaction data is secured by Google.
